Integrating factor

Any linear first-order ODE can be rearranged into the form

$$y'+p(x)y=g(x)$$
$$\mu(t)=e^{-\int p(t)dt}$$
$$y = \frac1{\mu(t)}\left[\int_{t_0}^t\mu(s)g(s)\,\mathrm ds+c\right]$$
